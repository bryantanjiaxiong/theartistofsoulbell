Pictures of My Family Video Calls

The period between 1500 hours and 1800 hours is the very time I look forward to daily. No doubt it is true that I stress about not being able to make a video call somedays, but it is a routine I will never betray from my first day in Galway to my last day here, for when the latter arrives I will never leave my home island for so long ever again. Dyllon and Chloe are always usually lazing around, refusing to do their schoolwork, and this I no doubt understand and support. I can usually see 公公 and 奶奶 on Sundays, and 婆婆 on Tuesdays to Thursdays. So far, I haven't had a chance to speak with Aunt Cat or 舅舅 yet, but 叔叔 and 婶婶 do call me sometimes. Mom and Dad are ever ready to give me advice on many things in my daily routine, and much more, I will always be thankful for that, though sometimes I have to apologize for losing my temper. It's not their fault, it's no one's but mine own. But every day, reaching evening, my spirits will be set alight once more because of these video calls, and me offering my eternal gratitude to my family does not do these hours justice.

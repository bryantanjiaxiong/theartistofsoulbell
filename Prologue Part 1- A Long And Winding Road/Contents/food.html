Pictures of My Food

From fruits to rice to pasta to cake, meals are not an issue for me as I have amazing chefs as friends here. I spent my first week in Galway alone in my unit, and so there were times when I had meals on the dining table by myself, with food like fruits and burgers. After those few days of eating alone, I found people to rely on for food, given that I pay for the ingredients, of course. There is Cindy who is my unit neighbour, Jeremy who is of the same course as her, and Cuong, a French Vietnamese who is also my unit neighbour. The latter is from a family of chefs and so has a higher skill level of cooking. He offered me cake and sushi before, and his food tastes marvellous. Cindy and Jeremy are always my mealmates, and we sing along to songs usually after dinner, procrastinating the need to return to our units to sleep. These will no doubt be good memories to look back on come the future
